Maximizing a view or editor

You can temporarily maximize a view or editor so that it fills the workbench window.

  1. Open the menu for the view or editor by clicking the icon at the far left of the view's or editor's title bar. (You can get the same effect by right-clicking elsewhere in the title bar.)

  2. Select Maximize.

Restore the maximized view in the same manner.

Tip: You can also maximize or restore a view or editor by double-clicking its title bar.
