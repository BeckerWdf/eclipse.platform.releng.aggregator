Automatically Switch to Team Perspective

You can choose whether to automatically switch to the Team perspective when a synchronization operation is performed:

1.

From the workbench, select the Window > Preferences menu item.

2.

In the preferences dialog, select the Team page in the left pane.

3.

Select the "Switch to team perspective on catch up/release" checkbox, if you want to switch to the Team perspective when a synchronization operation is performed.
4.  Click the "OK" button of the Preferences dialog.
