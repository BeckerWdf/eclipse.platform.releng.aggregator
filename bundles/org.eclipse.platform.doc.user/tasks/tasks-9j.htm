Reset perspectives

To reset a perspective to its original layout proceed as follows:

1.

Choose Window > Preferences  

2.

Expand Workbench and choose Perspectives.

3.

 Select the perspective and click the Reset button and then Click OK.

4. We have updated the saved state of the perspective to match its original state. If we also want to update the current copy of the perspective (if it is open) then  we must also choose Perspective > Reset from the workbench's menubar.
