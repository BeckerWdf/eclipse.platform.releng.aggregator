Creating floating views

This information applies only to Windows.

Views can be "torn off" from the workbench window and can "float" on the desktop on their own. Even though a view is floating it continues to behave as a part of the perspective from which it was detached. For example, if you hide a perspective that contains a floating view, the floating view is hidden as well.

To launch a view in a secondary window:

  1. Drag the view by its title bar to an area outside the current window.

  2. After dropping the view in its new location, resize the view by dragging its borders.
