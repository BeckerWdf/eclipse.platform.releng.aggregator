Changing the placement of editor tabs

You can change whether the tabs for stacked editors appear at the top or bottom of the editor area.

  1. On the main menu bar, click Window > Preferences.

  2. Expand the Workbench category and select Appearance.

  3. Use the radio buttons in the Editor tab positions group to control whether you want the tabs for open files to appear at the top or the bottom of the editor area.

  4. Click Apply and OK.

  5. Close and reopen the workbench to in order to see the changes.
