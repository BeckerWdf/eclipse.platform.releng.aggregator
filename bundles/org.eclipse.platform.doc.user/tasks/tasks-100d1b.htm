Overriding patterns that prevent resources from releasing

After you have added global file-matching patterns to the list of files to be ignored at release time, you may wish to occasionally override the "ignore."

  1. From the main menu bar, select Workbench > Preferences > Team > Ignored Resources.

  2. To temporarily override one or more of the file-matching patterns, clear their checkboxes.

  3. Click OK.

Files that match the patterns you have deselected will not be ignored during catch-up or release operations.

To permanently remove a pattern, select it and click Remove.
