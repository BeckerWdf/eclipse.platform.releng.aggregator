Replacing perspectives

In addition to opening new perspectives you can also replace the current perspective. This effectively alters the layout of the current perspective. Editors that were open continue to remain open in the new perspective.  

To replace the current perspective hold down Ctrl or Shift-Alt (depending on your operating system) while opening the perspective. 

You can change your workbench preferences (Workbench > Preferences > Workbench) to specify whether you want to replace the current perspective when you open projects that would otherwise open a different perspective.
