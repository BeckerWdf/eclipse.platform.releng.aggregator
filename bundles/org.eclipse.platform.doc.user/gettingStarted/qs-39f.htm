Rearranging tabbed views

In addition to dragging and dropping views on the workbench you can also rearrange the order of views within a tabbed notebook.

1.

Choose Perspective > Reset to reset the Resource perspective back to its original layout. 

2.

Click on the Navigator tab and drag it to the right of the Repositories tab.

3.

Once your cursor is to the right of the Repositories tab and the cursor is a stack cursor release the mouse button.

Observe the Navigator tab is now to the right of the Repositories tab.
