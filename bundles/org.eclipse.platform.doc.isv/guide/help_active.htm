Active help

Active help is the ability to invoke a workbench action from on-line documentation. 

Instead of writing, "Go to the Window Menu and open the Preferences Dialog," your on-line help can include a link that will open the Preferences Dialog for the user.  Active help links look like hyperlinks in the on-line help.
